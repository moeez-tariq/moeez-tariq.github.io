DATES ARE NUTRIENT RICH

Three dates provide about 200 calories, 54 grams of carbohydrates with about five grams of fiber, a gram of protein, and no fat. This portion also supplies smaller amounts of a wide range of nutrients, including B vitamins, vitamin K, calcium, iron, magnesium, potassium, zinc, and manganese.

OTHER RECIPES TO TRY..!

'Avocado Toast'

Toast a slice of bread. Mash 1/2 an avocado and spread it on the toast. Top with a fried or poached egg, and sprinkle with salt and pepper to taste.
