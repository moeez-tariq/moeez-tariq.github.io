THE STORY OF THE GREEDY DOG!

There was a dog that was very hungry.

He searched for food everywhere and eventually he found a bone.

He was very proud of his find and would carry it everywhere he went.

While going home, he stopped at a riverbank and looked at his reflection.

He thought there is another dog with a bone in its mouth in the water.

Looking at this, he growled at the second dog.

He dropped his bone and jumped into the water, but he soon realized his mistake.

There was no other dog, just his own reflection.

He now tried to find the bone he had dropped in the water.

But he realized that the current had taken the bone away forever.

So he swam and swam till he reached the shore.

The dog sat on the shore drying himself.

He had lost everything he had because of his greed.

Now once again he was sad, empty-handed and hungry.

MORAL OF THE STORY: DONT BE GREEDY!
